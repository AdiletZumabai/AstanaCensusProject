Budget Overview

Efficient Allocation of Resources for Project Success

Budget Breakdown

Our budget for the Astana Census 2024 project is allocated to ensure that all phases are adequately resourced for successful implementation:

Technical Infrastructure

20-25% of the budget is dedicated to developing and supporting platforms, mobile applications, and servers essential for the census.

Salaries of Employees

30-35% of the budget goes towards salaries for project managers, IT specialists, analysts, and field workers.

Marketing & Awareness Campaigns

10-15% of the budget is allocated for advertising and awareness efforts to encourage public participation.

Hardware & Software

15-20% is reserved for purchasing necessary hardware, such as tablets and servers, and software licenses.

Reserve Fund

5-10% is set aside as a contingency fund to address unforeseen challenges, such as delays or technical issues.
