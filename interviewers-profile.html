Interviewers' Profile

Roles, Requirements, and Benefits for Our Census Team

Role Requirements

Skills

Strong communication, technical knowledge, and attention to detail are essential for accurate data collection.

Experience

Prior data collection experience is preferred, ensuring familiarity with the challenges of gathering accurate information.

Recruitment & Training

We recruit a diverse group, including government employees, students, and volunteers. Our training program covers key topics essential for effective data collection.

Step 1: Digital Tools Training

Instruction on using digital tools and data collection devices to ensure accuracy and efficiency.

Step 2: Conflict Management

Guidance on managing conflicts and engaging with respondents professionally.

Step 3: Survey Question Clarity

In-depth review of survey questions to ensure they are clear, accurate, and easily understood.
