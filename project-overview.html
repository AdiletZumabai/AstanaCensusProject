Project Overview

Understanding the Astana Census 2024

Project Scope

The Astana Census 2024 aims to conduct a full census of Astana’s population using advanced data collection tools and AI-powered analytics. The project focuses on gathering accurate demographic data to support the city's development and planning needs.

Project Phases

Month 1

Initial Planning: Defining scope, allocating resources, and setting up infrastructure.

Month 2

Development and Pilot Survey: Creating offline data tools and conducting pilot tests.

Month 3

Full-Scale Data Collection – Initiating large-scale data collection with constant monitoring and support.

Month 4

Data Validation and Quality Control – Ensuring the accuracy of collected data through quality control checks.

Month 5

Data Analysis – Analyzing the validated data to extract useful insights and preparing preliminary reports.

Month 6

Final Reporting and Delivery – Compiling the final report with findings and recommendations, marking the project’s conclusion.
