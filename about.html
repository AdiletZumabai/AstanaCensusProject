About CensusTech Solutions

Your partner in delivering accurate and reliable census data for Astana.

Company Profile

Founded: 2019

Overview: CensusTech Solutions specializes in large-scale data collection and demographic analysis. With expertise in Central Asia, our team provides innovative census solutions for government agencies, private organizations, and international bodies. We ensure data accuracy by leveraging mobile applications, cloud databases, and AI-powered analytics.

Our Mission

To deliver efficient and reliable census solutions through digital data collection, supporting strategic planning and policy-making with accurate population data. We aim to lead in digital census management by using advanced technology and optimized processes.

Our Team

Executive Team

Our executive team, including the CEO, COO, and CFO, oversees high-level decision-making, strategy, and client relationships, ensuring financial and operational success.

Project Management

Led by a Head of Projects, the project management team coordinates census activities. Regional project managers ensure each phase runs smoothly and on schedule.

Data Collection Team

Field coordinators and data collectors gather census data using mobile applications and cloud systems for efficient data collection.

Data Analysis and IT Team

Our technical team processes census data using AI-powered analytics and ensures data integrity and security throughout the project.
